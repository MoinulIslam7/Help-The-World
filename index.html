Our Donate Plan

We have some different type of donation plan. Mainly we are focusing on food, shelter and education. Because this are most needed criteria for a human. You can donate any of them.


food for family

Enough food exists in the world to feed everyone, yet people still suffer from food insecurity. If you share this belief, join us in the fight against hunger with a generous gift today. Your donation of as little as 100tk per day will feed a family.

shelter for a family

Helping those persons who do not have a proper home, no shelter or have depleted shelter, live in insanitary conditions. Many of them sleep on streets, subways, footpaths, doorways or park benches under the open sky.

Education for a child

Education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. supporting child survival, growth, development and well-being and closing the gap in social inequality. On average one additional year of education can increase an individual's earnings by 10 per cent.
